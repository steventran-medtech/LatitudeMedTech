# Business Operations

## Business Lines
1. **Coaching (Active)** — MedTech career coaching, resume reviews,
   interview prep, LinkedIn optimization, career strategy
2. **Consulting (Gated)** — Regulatory advisory; held until non-compete
   cleared and RAC consultant under contract

## Coaching Workflow
1. Client books via Calendly
2. Steve runs `/project:new-client` or Coaching Brief Agent manually
3. Agent generates discovery brief from client name/LinkedIn
4. Steve reviews brief before call — never skip this step
5. Post-call: content agent can draft follow-up or resources
6. All AI outputs reviewed by Steve before sending to client

## Content & Marketing
- Publication: MedTech Meridian (Substack + LinkedIn)
- Cadence: Weekly draft generated by Content Agent
- Steve approves every piece before publishing — no autopublish
- Content topics: regulatory affairs, career development, MedTech industry

## Client Rules

- No cross-client data sharing under any circumstances
- All deliverables marked with readiness label and disclaimer
- Steve is the sole client-facing representative at Alpha/Beta stage
