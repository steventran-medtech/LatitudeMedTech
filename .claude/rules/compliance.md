# Compliance, Legal & Security

## Hard Stops — Never Override
- All outputs = AI-assisted support tools. NOT licensed regulatory advice.
- Licensed RAC consultant must review ALL consulting client deliverables.
- No PHI until HIPAA BAA is signed. No exceptions.
- No consulting client work until non-compete is attorney-confirmed cleared.
- No ISO standard text (13485, 14971, 62304) ingested verbatim into RAG.
- No secrets in code, git, or chat. Keys in `Athena/voice/.env` only.
- RBAC required before any client can access the system (Phase 3+).

## Output Labeling — Required on Every Output
Every agent output must be explicitly labeled:
- **Demo** — proof of concept only, not for any real use
- **Alpha** — internal use, Steve reviews 100% before acting on it
- **Beta** — limited client use, SME review required
- **Production** — full controls in place (not yet reached)

## Disclaimer — Append to All Outputs
> "This output was generated by an AI assistant as a support tool for
> internal review purposes only. It does not constitute licensed
> regulatory affairs advice. All client-facing deliverables must be
> reviewed and approved by a licensed regulatory affairs professional
> (RAC-qualified or equivalent) before delivery. Latitude MedTech LLC
> and its principals retain sole responsibility for all regulatory
> submissions and compliance decisions."

## Regulatory Frameworks in Scope
- FDA: 21 CFR Part 820, 510(k), PMA, De Novo, EUA
- EU: MDR 2017/745, IVDR
- ISO: 13485:2016, 14971:2019, 62304
- MDSAP audit program

## Data Rules
- All client data isolated by client_id at DB level from day one.
- No client data in any shared or test environment.
- Data retention and deletion policy required before Phase 3.
- EU clients trigger GDPR data subject rights requirements.
- Export control review required before any dual-use tech advisory.
