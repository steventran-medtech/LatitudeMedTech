# Latitude MedTech LLC
## Phase 1A Guerilla Marketing & Sales Plan
### 30-60-90 Day Sprint to First Paying Coaching Clients
**Prepared by:** Marketing & Sales Director
**Date:** June 5, 2026
**Classification:** Internal — Executive Review
**Disclaimer:** All external content produced under this plan must carry the standard Alpha-phase disclaimer: *"For educational purposes only; not regulatory advice."*

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Single Quantified Target:** 3 paying coaching clients by September 3, 2026 (Day 90), generating a minimum of $4,500 in recognized coaching revenue, validating Athena's commercial viability and establishing the proof points required to open the Consulting (Phase 1B) gate.

**Strategic Logic:** Latitude MedTech has no brand equity, no case studies, and no paid ad budget. The path to first revenue runs entirely through *earned trust* — built in corridors, inboxes, and microphones, not in ad auctions. Every action in this plan either (a) builds credibility with a specific named target, (b) generates a first-party introduction, or (c) converts an introduction into a booked discovery call. Nothing else ships.

**Current pipeline:** 20 identified opportunities across 6 channels. Zero are qualified. Conversion assumption: 20% of identified → qualified conversation; 25% of qualified conversation → paid client. To reach 3 clients, we need ≥60 identified opportunities or dramatic yield improvement on existing ones. Both levers are pulled simultaneously below.

**Plan Owner:** Steven Tran (CEO), supported by Marketing & Sales Director for content, sequencing, and logistics.
