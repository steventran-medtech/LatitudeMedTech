# Test Brief: Medium Length Filename

This is a test brief with a medium-length filename. Used to verify that the ellipsis truncation activates at the right threshold — the title should be partially visible with a trailing ellipsis.

## Purpose

Confirm that `textOverflow: ellipsis` and `whiteSpace: nowrap` kick in correctly for filenames that are borderline fits within the column width.
